The Knight of Shadows:Between Yin and Yang (2019)

The barrier protecting humans from the realm of demons has fallen! Hordes of demons are pouring into the human realm. The King of Demons sends demon hunter Songling (Jackie Chan) in the guise of a master detective to track down every last demon. Gifted with the king's magical calligraphy brush, he is also aided on his mission by a motley group of friendly demons. He teams up with police detective Fei (Austin Lin) to solve the case of a series of teenage village girls who have gone missing.

He learns that the girls are being abducted by the beautiful demoness Xiaoqian (Elaine Zhong) in partnership with a duplicitous mirror demon (Lin Peng). As Songling investigates, he encounters another demon hunter, Chixia (Ethan Juan), in mid-battle with two demons.
