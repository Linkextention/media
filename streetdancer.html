Street Dancer (2020)

Inder Singh Narula (Punit Pathak) is a dancer who is part of a dance team named Street Dancer. Inder is dancing at the Ground Zero Battle with his brother Sahej Singh Narula (Varun Dhawan) cheering for him and while doing a flip stunt, he lands on his leg and injures it. After two years, he is healing, but cannot dance. Sahej forms Street Dancer again and converts it into a studio through money whose sources he is hesitant about revealing. Poddy (Raghav Juyal), is one of Sahej's close friends, along with D (Dharmesh Yelande) and Sushi (Sushant Pujari), who are all part of Street Dancer.
