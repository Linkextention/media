Chhichhore (2019)

At present, Aniruddh "Anni" Pathak is a divorced middle-aged man living with his teenage son Raghav, an aspiring engineer, who is awaiting the results of his entrance examination in the hope of enrolling in an Indian Institute of Technology (IIT). On the eve of the results, Aniruddh gifts Raghav a bottle of champagne, promising him that they will celebrate his success together, unaware that Raghav is under intense pressure. The bottle of champagne only fuels Raghav's worries that he might not make it.

The next day, while checking the results at his apartment, Raghav finds out that he has indeed failed his exams and, afraid of being called a "loser", he jumps off a balcony.
